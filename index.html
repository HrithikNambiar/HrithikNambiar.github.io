I am a junior year undergraduate pursuing a major in Computer Science and a minor in Data Science at BITS Pilani, India .

My research interests lie in Artificial Intelligence specifically in Deep learning, Computer Vision and Neuroscience. I am also passionate for mathematics and problem solving.

At BITS, I am affiliated wih APPCAIR and work in collaboration with TCS Research. Previously, I was a undergraduate researcher at the Cognitive Neuroscience lab at BITS Pilani. As a core member in the Society for Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning, I have been involved in various projects and conducting and teaching some student run courses related to AI.
